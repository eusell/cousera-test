Jongksha Village

Jongksha Village is located in Mawkynrew Block, East Khasi Hills district, in the state of Meghalaya, India, with a 32,10 people population. The village has its own post office and 793015 is the pin code of the village of Jongksha. The district headquarters of Mawkynrew is 8 km from the village. Shillong, which is 35 km from the district headquarters of the town. The village is home to 3210 people, 1595 (50%) of whom are males and 1615 (50%) are females. 1 % of the population is general caste and 99% are programming tribes. The child population (under six years of age) of the village of Jongksha is 23%, 53% of whom are youth, 47% females. In the village there are 573 families and in each family there are an average of 6 individuals.
